Perfect Roast Chicken

A classic roast chicken recipe that's simple to make and delivers amazing results every time. Crispy skin, juicy meat, and beautiful flavors.

Prep Time

15 mins

Cook Time

1 hour 20 mins

Serves

4 people

Difficulty

Method

Preheat oven

5 mins

Preheat your oven to 200C/400F. Remove the chicken from the fridge 30 minutes before cooking to bring it to room temperature.

Prepare chicken

5 mins

Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. This helps achieve crispy skin.

Stuff chicken

5 mins

Cut the lemon in half and place inside the cavity along with the garlic bulb (cut in half crosswise) and herbs.

Season chicken

5 mins

Rub the chicken all over with olive oil and season generously with salt and pepper, including inside the cavity.

Prepare vegetables

5 mins

Place the prepared vegetables in a roasting tray and place the chicken on top.

Roast chicken

80 mins

Roast for 1 hour 20 minutes, or until the juices run clear when you pierce the thickest part of the thigh.

Rest and serve

15 mins

Rest the chicken for 15 minutes before carving. Serve with the roasted vegetables and pan juices.

Chef's Tips

Bringing the chicken to room temperature helps it cook more evenly

Don't skip the resting time - it helps keep the meat juicy

Save the bones to make homemade chicken stock

For extra flavor, stuff some herb butter under the skin before roasting

Baste the chicken every 20-30 minutes while roasting for extra juicy meat

Use a meat thermometer to ensure the chicken is cooked through - it should read 75C/165F in the thickest part

Let the chicken rest breast-side down to help the juices flow back into the meat

Recipe Variations

Lemon & Herb

A citrusy twist on the classic recipe

  • Add extra lemon zest and juice
  • Use a mix of fresh herbs like parsley, tarragon, and chives
  • Add white wine to the roasting pan

Spicy Version

A spicier take with added heat

  • Add paprika and cayenne to the seasoning
  • Include chopped chili in the cavity
  • Serve with spicy gravy

Ingredients

4
  • 1 whole chicken (about 1.6kg)
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • Fresh herbs (thyme, rosemary)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 onions, quartered
  • 4 carrots, chunked
  • 4 potatoes, quartered
  • 2 celery sticks, chunked
